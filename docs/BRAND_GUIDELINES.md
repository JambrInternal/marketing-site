# Brand Guidelines

## Personality

- Practical
- Calm
- Reliable
- No hype

Think: the best dispatcher you’ve ever worked with.

## Voice & tone

- Short sentences
- Plain language
- Outcome-focused
- Respectful of blue-collar work

Avoid:
- Buzzwords
- “Revolutionary”
- “Next-gen”
- Overpromising

## Color direction (reference only)

- Deep navy
- Steel blue
- Neutral grays
- Minimal accents

Visuals should feel:
- Industrial
- Trustworthy
- Clean

Brand assets live in `public/brand` and `public/favicon`.
